Moodologist


HackFSU Spring 2015 | Team Project | Responsible for Framework and Animations (jQuery)

Dates: March 20th - 22nd, 2015
|
marymkelly.github.io/mood-mixer

Home Page

Moodologist is the project that my HackFSU team did in 30 hours. A friend of ours said we should build a site that recommends a drink based off your moood. People can choose from colors, emojis, landscapes, and words to determine the way they are feeling, and the site spits out a drink recommendation. The site still has a few kinks, but we felt it was better to leave it as is to demonstrate the amount of work we did in 30 hours. I was responsible for writing the framework, some of the design, and all of the of the animations.
